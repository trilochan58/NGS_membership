Membership Guidelines

At present, there are two individual membership and one institutional membership categories in Nepal Geotechnical Society:

  1. Regular (General) Member
  2. Life Member
  3. Student Member
  4. Institutional Member (Corporate Member)

The following is the membership fee structure:

Membership Type Fees Detail
Regular (General) Membership First time registration fee: NRs. 2,000 and annual renewal fee: NRs. 1,000
Life Membership Registration fee: NRs. 10,000,
Student Membership First time registration fee: NRs. 1,000 and annual renewal fee: NRs. 500
Institutional Membership Note: Institutional Membership (Corporate Members) is further divided into three categories: 1. General Corporate Support, 2. Gold Corporate Support, and 3. Platinum Corporate Support. For details click here.

If you would like to register with Nepal Geotechnical Society as its potential member, you may fill out a preliminary membership application form available in the top button link and send to us for further process and registration fee payment procedure.

Thank you.
